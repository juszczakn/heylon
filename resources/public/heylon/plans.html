Plans

Overview

Heylon plans to be an MMO based on the likes of Shadowmere.

For those unfamiliar, this implies:

  • Heylon will be turn based, with player turns created every hour.
  • Each player will be in control of one kingdom.
    • The player will control which race and location their kingdom has.
      Both of these will have a large impact on how the game will be played.
    • Each kingdom will be composed of multiple villages. A village
      exists on a single parcel of land.
  • The goal of the game is to expand your kingdom, however that may be.
    • War
    • Trade
    • Diplomacy
  • Races will have different bonuses, which will lend themselves to different playstyles.
